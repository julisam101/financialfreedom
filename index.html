Your First 3 Steps

Quick Start
  1. 1
    Review last month

    Write down your income and expenses. No judgment — just an honest look at the numbers.

  2. 2
    Set one goal

    Choose a single, concrete target for this month — such as saving $50 or making an extra debt payment.

  3. 3
    Check in weekly

    Spend 10 minutes each week reviewing your progress and adjusting your plan as needed.
